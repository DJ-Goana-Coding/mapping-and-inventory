## Metrics

### Health Score Calculation
```
Critical = 10 points penalty
High     = 5 points penalty
Medium   = 2 points penalty
Low      = 1 point penalty

Health Score = 100 - Total Penalty
```

### Coverage Calculation
```
Coverage % = (Passed Checks / Total Checks) × 100
```

### Target
**100% Coverage** with **Health Score ≥ 90**
