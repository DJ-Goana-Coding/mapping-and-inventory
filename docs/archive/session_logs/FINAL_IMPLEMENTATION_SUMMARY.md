Each solution rated on:
- **Effort Score** (1-10, lower is faster)
- **Risk Score** (1-10, lower is safer)
- **Cost** (free vs. paid)
- **Implementation Time** (hours estimate)
- **Tools Required** (list)
- **Steps** (implementation procedure)
